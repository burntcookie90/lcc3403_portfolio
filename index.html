    MA2 blog post

    Mar 1, 2013 11:16 AM

    I think the best thing about my submission is the instructional video that I recorded. It takes everything from the original source instructions and puts it in an easy to understand, short, format. The hardest part of creating it was coming up with ways to improve a set of instructions that were already pretty good to start with. I visualized my reader as someone similar to me: good technical knowledge, but average to low communication knowledge. By doing this, I was able to voice my ideas and concerns as in a more natural manner. My objective was to point out some flaws and areas for improvement in the existing instructions, an objective that I think I met. Overall, I think I deserve a B to B+ on this assignment. Although I feel that I met my own personal objective, I could have had more content and I am not very confident about my grammar skills.
